CARK Technique

Created by Hibatulloh Mohammad Restu

Date Created: 10 May 2024  Location: Indonesia

What is CARK?

CARK stands for "Campuran Merk" or "Mixed Brands." It is a coloring technique that blends various brands of colored pencils to achieve a rich and realistic finish. This approach embraces the strengths of each brand, using them for different purposes like blending, contrast, and texture.

Background

Born out of necessity and limited tools, the CARK technique was developed through creative problem-solving. It proves that unique and beautiful results can come from combining what we already have.

Philosophy

CARK is more than just a technique. It carries a message: no matter your tools, you can create something extraordinary through persistence and passion. Every pencil, no matter the brand, has value.

Brands Used

  • Faber-Castell (Classic / Polychromos)
  • Joyko
  • Deli Oil
  • Other local brands

Goals

The goal of CARK is to inspire young artists to embrace what they have. By doing so, they can develop their own unique styles and express themselves without limits.
